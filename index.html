Virtual Learning Enviroment

                  As students, we decided to focus on a web app that will be useful to students for this project I went through several Learning management systems (LMS) and noticed they did not feature many tools to tackle one of the main problems students face: scheduling, or making a time table and setting personal reminders
                  As a student, I have personally faced a lot of problems during the online phase related to managing assignments, quizzes, and semester projects. I didn’t have personal reminders of the things mentioned and also I couldn’t make a timetable for the subjects I had. These things had made my study slow and I got fewer grades than I expected.
                   So I would like to come up with the solution to these things in the website I will be making. I will be making a leaning management system of my own that will help the students with problems I faced during online where they can pre-schedule their task projects and assignments and can also have a time table for their subjects and can set personal reminders. So they can manage their time for studies wisely to get better grades and makes things efficient and smooth.
                  In addition, I will be adding the E-commerce facility in my web application that will be related to buying and teaching online courses within the learning management system (LMS).
                  How we will create this is that we will be adding several courses related to the student’s subjects some could be free and some they can purchase and that course will be pre-scheduled according to the timetable will help the students that are using the learning management system to get deeper and better understanding of the subject.
                  We will be making this web application in the course of this semester and at the end, we will be presenting it after our final papers and we will be purchasing a domain name and hosting for our LMS
